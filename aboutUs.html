Our Philosophy

Maximize the look and feel of Your Home

Bella Design and Staging’s goal is to inspire our clients everyday moments by creating a thematic inspiration and timeless personal space. Our designers re-imagine a space that allows prospective buyers to visualize and connect with a homes’ potential beauty.

We assist Realtors and homeowners in Marin and Southern Sonoma Counties in achieving a high level design with flawless execution and impeccable service.

Bella Design and Staging has high quality contemporary furnishings and various accents to get your home market ready. By staging a home it opens up prospective buyer’s minds to visually see themselves in your home.

Studies conducted by the National Association of Realtors reveals that only ten percent of buyers visualize the potential of a home that is not staged and that leaving a home “as is” actually helps the competition. Research has also proven that home staging has brought in an average of 8-10% higher sales prices and helps cut the average days the home is on the market. Staged homes look better than the competition and cost much less than your first price reduction.
