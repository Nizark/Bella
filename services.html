We Offer Three Premium Home Design and Staging Packages

Professional home staging for real estate agents and homeowners.

Staging will add considerable Appeal and Value to Your Home

Pegeusis Package:

Whole House Staging with one of our designer collections

Completed in Four Easy and Stress-Free Steps.

Step One: we would do a Walk Through

We will schedule a one-hour onsite visit to discuss design and client goals.

Step Two: Proposal

This will include our recommended furniture, design plan, duration, dates and pricing.

Step Three: Staging

When proposal is agreed upon, staging of the space will commence on the date specified and will typically take one to two days.Our contracts are generally for a period of 30 to 60 days, with an option to extend.

Step Four: Removal

Once your closing date is set, we will schedule the breakdown of staging, which typically takes one day.

Our recommendations for placement and removal of existing furniture.

Step Three: Re-Design and De-Clutter

Rooms will be transformed, typically one day process.
